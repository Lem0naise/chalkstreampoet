PLANETIN PERIL

As the challenges of the climate crisis become ever clearer, we are also facing a Biodiversity Crisis. X% of our mammals, x% of our birds, and x% insects are under severe threat of extinction, with species having become extinct in the last decade alone. These rates are accelerating. Planet in Peril provides a series of thought-provoking toys, games and interactive displays which will set your students pondering

  • what is happening to our biodiversity
  • the implications of this for humans, and
  • what we can do to help prevent it.
