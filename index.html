Planet in Peril!

About Us
Planet in Peril is a workshop for schools in the UK, where the students have an hour and a half to explore a series of thought-provoking toys, games and interactive displays which will set them pondering
  • What is happening to our biodiversity,
  • The implications of this for humans, and
  • What we can do to help prevent it.
